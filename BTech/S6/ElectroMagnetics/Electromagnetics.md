#### Magnetic Flux
$$
\phi_B = \int \overrightarrow B \ . d \overrightarrow A
$$
where
- $\phi_B$ ->  `Magnetic Flux`
- $\overrightarrow B$ -> `Magnetic Field`
- $d\overrightarrow A$ -> `Local Vector`

$$
E  = {d {\phi_B} \over dt}
$$
